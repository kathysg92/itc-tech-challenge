About Women in Business

This program addresses the need among multinationals and larger enterprises to engage with companies owned by women. By developing a digital tool more corporations will be able to include more women-owned businesses – especially from developing countries – in their supply chains. Around the globe, women do 70% of the world's work and earn only 10% of the income. They own 1/3 of all private businesses and earn only 1% of corporate and government procurement spend. Within this context, the International Trade Centre and partners have a goal to support 1 million WOBs enter international markets by 2020. The development of this digital tool will support this effort to create a more efficient, effective channel to identify, develop, and scale high-potential women entrepreneurs who could become strong corporate suppliers.
