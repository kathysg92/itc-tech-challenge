Asistente Virtual CR is a new start up virtual assistant business, serving two different market segments. The first customer segment is companies of various sizes that are initiating operations or are downsizing. In many cases they need to outsource administrative support and project management, which the current staff cannot handle. Second customer segment is individuals that need to free time to invest in managing their business operations.

My work experience in the Canadian Embassy, Ministry of Foreign Affairs, CINDE and Intel Corporation, brought me a wealth of expertise and knowledge to offer my best to both market segments. In alignment with this vision, I believe in the power of being part of a strong networking system. For this reason I became a certified member of We Connect International, in August 2014. I had the opportunity to meet a local business event with vendors, where I got my first opportunity, to make business with a multinational company.

In March 2015, I became a business provider of the U.S. Embassy in San José, Costa Rica. This allowed me to engage with new contacts to expand my networking and business. Then, I was offered the opportunity to apply and been accepted in a mentorship program promoted by the Cherie Blair Foundation. I have an extraordinary mentor, who is helping me to grow in the business level and the personal one.

At present, I am supporting the fourth more important company in telecommunications in Costa Rica. Working in parallel with my clients, I understand them, their business needs and create the proposals to achieve their goals and implement them. The way I make plans real, by doing whatever it takes to help customers to achieve their goals, is paramount and gives me a lot of passion for what I do as a business woman.

To continue growing my business, I attend events and venues such as breakfasts, conferences and meetings with suppliers. Moving to a more international environment, I was selected to attend the Women Vendors Exhibition and Forum in Sao Paulo, Brazil on the 2 and 3 September, organized by the International Trade Centre. This event offers unparalleled opportunities for me to attract new business, new partnerships, learn, grow and expand to new markets.
